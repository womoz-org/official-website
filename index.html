About Us

About Us

The WoMoz(Women in Mozilla) is an organization that focuses exclusively on increasing the degree of
women's participation and visibility in Mozilla and open-source projects.

we believe that women can actively engage in open source project without considering facts like being a woman an being very young or very old.

Women & Mozilla ("WoMoz") is a community that give aspiration to the female members to connect, contribute and collaborate in various open source projects.

Our Vision is to:

  • Energize and motivate our existing community of users and women contributors.
  • Increase mindshare and drive product downloads
  • Lay the seeds for new women contributions and identify next generation of women contributors
  • Leverage and extend our reach internationally and beyond targeted locations through easily reproducible events.

Aims

Diminish the Gender Gap
